About us

Auto Protector is the leader in ceramic coatings based on nano technology. We offer a range of products each specifically used for different surfaces. Our ceramic coatings are used in the automotive industry for cars and bikes. The different surfaces it can be applied on is paint, glass, plastics, fabric, leather, wheels, etc. Our coatings bond with the different surfaces at a molecular level filling in all nanopores and hence creates a hydrophobic surface that is impervious to contamination.
